## Inferred (not directly verified)
- !Dead("resref") evaluates TRUE for a creature that was never created (death counter 0) — hence BDFIST01/05 escort blocks and BDSCHAEL.baf night blocks would run, not stay dormant, if their placed CREs exist without the fight. Standard IE engine behavior, not tested on this install.
- The BD0100.are placed night set (Assassin1-3/Corwin/FF Guards/corpses) is present on area load whenever the area is first entered before bd_plot reaches 52; vanilla ordering just never lets the player in before the night. Inferred from ARE actor list (10c) + absence of any activation gate in BD0100.baf.
- KHALIJ.dlg states 207-208 (bd_plot=260 betrayal discussion) appear to be Beamdog-shipped content reachable only when Khalid is in the party mid-SoD, i.e. via EET/continuity mods or dead vanilla code — not traced to a spawner.
- The EE quest system (bgee.lua createQuest/createEntry) tolerates entries that are never added — a quest whose opener strref never fires simply starts at whatever entry arrives first; so cutting 264565/266718/266840/267494 leaves no orphaned journal UI state.
- BDCAELAR states 12-16 belong to the chapter-8 parley at the coalition camp (BD1000) based on BDCUT15 being launched from BD1000.baf:335 and the 'Return to Baldur's Gate. Tell the Council' content; the exact chapter number label was not re-verified against chapter globals.
- Edwin/Corwin/crusader 'she tried to kill me' replies (list c) are pure flavor with no mechanical reads — no Global checks on any assassination flag were found in their triggers; removing or rewording them is text-only work.
